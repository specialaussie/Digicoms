Rental Karting Tips and tricks

Rental karts 9 times out of 10 are sodi kart RT 10's, an entry level racing kart that is limited on speed and power (unfortunately) based on the place your racing at.

The Sodikarts have a specific way of racing them, they require small presice movements to get smooth movement around corners. also throttle control is a must for these karts.
